Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about blood donation and using our platform.

Who can donate blood? expand_more

Generally, you can donate blood if you:

  • Are at least 18 years old
  • Weigh at least 50 kg
  • Are in good health
  • Haven't donated blood in the last 3 months

Some medical conditions and medications may disqualify you from donating. Check with your healthcare provider if you're unsure.

How does BloodConnect protect my privacy? expand_more

We take privacy seriously. When someone needs to contact a donor:

  • They submit a request through our platform
  • We notify the donor about the request
  • The donor can choose to accept or decline
  • If accepted, we facilitate communication without sharing direct contact information initially
  • Both parties can choose to share more details directly if they wish

Your personal information is encrypted and never shared without your explicit consent.

How do I verify my blood group? expand_more

To verify your blood group on our platform:

  • Upload a recent blood test report from a recognized laboratory
  • Or upload a donor card from a blood bank or hospital
  • Or upload a certificate from a registered medical professional

Our team will review the document and verify your blood group within 24-48 hours.

Is there a fee for using BloodConnect? expand_more

No, BloodConnect is completely free for both donors and recipients. Our mission is to save lives by connecting people who need blood with willing donors, without any financial barriers.

We are supported by grants and donations to maintain our platform and services.
